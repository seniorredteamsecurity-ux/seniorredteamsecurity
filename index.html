Frequently Asked Questions

Is the physical security testing legal?
Yes, 100%. We only conduct physical penetration tests with written authorization from the property owner or authorized representative. We provide a detailed scope document and "get out of jail free" letter for our operators during engagements.
Can I buy hardware tools without a pentest engagement?
Yes. We sell hardware tools to verified security professionals, red teamers, and researchers. We require proof of legitimate use (employment, certification, or research purpose) before shipping specialized hardware.
How long does a typical engagement take?
Depends on scope. Phishing simulations: 1-2 weeks. Web pentest: 2-3 weeks. Red Team operation: 3-6 weeks. AI agent development: 2-8 weeks. Physical pentest: 1-3 days on-site. We give you a clear timeline upfront.
Are your certifications recognized by employers?
Our certifications are practical and assessment-based, designed to prove real skills. While newer than CEH or OSCP, they're built on modern AI-driven workflows that many employers are actively seeking. Each certificate has a unique verifiable ID.
Do you sign NDAs?
Absolutely. We sign NDAs before any engagement begins. All findings are shared through secure channels, and we follow strict data handling procedures. Your information never leaves our secure environment.
What kind of AI agents can you build?
Anything you can imagine: trading bots, customer support agents, code review assistants, security monitoring agents, data analysis bots, research agents, content generators, or multi-agent systems that work together. If it can be automated with AI, we can build it.
